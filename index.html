Our Story

Kampala Deserves a Table Worth Dressing For

Cantine Divino was born from a simple conviction: that Kampala's growing appetite for genuine luxury deserved more than hotel buffets and imported mediocrity.

Tucked into a candlelit garden on Mabua Road in Kololo, we've spent over a decade perfecting the marriage of Italian culinary tradition and East African warmth. Our pasta is shaped by hand each morning. Our steaks are sourced for marbling, not just weight. Our wine list is built by people who've walked the vineyards.

This is not a restaurant that tries to be Italian. It simply is.

What We Do Best

Three Passions.
One Table.

We don't do everything. We do three things with obsessive precision — and each one earns its place on your plate.

Signature · Aged Cuts

Fire & Patience

We source only prime cuts — T-bones, ribeyes, and sirloin — dry-aged to develop flavour depth you can taste in the first bite. Charcoal-finished, rested properly, served with reverence.

From Scratch · Every Morning

La Pasta

No dried packets. No shortcuts. Our pasta is hand-rolled each morning from semolina and farm eggs. Tossed with imported DOP ingredients and sauces that take all day to earn their depth.

Italian & International · Cellar

The Wine Experience

From Barolo to Brunello, from Prosecco to Primitivo — our cellar is a love letter to the Italian regions. Our team pairs every glass to your meal, not just your budget.
