Our Story

Not Trying
to Be Italian.
Simply Is.

Cantine Divino was founded on a conviction Kampala's growing appetite for genuine luxury dining deserved more than hotel restaurants and imported mediocrity.

Tucked into a candlelit garden on Mabua Road in Kololo, we've spent over a decade building something that doesn't need explaining — a place where the pasta is shaped by hand every morning, the steaks are chosen for marbling and not just weight, and the wine list reflects someone who has actually walked the Italian vineyards.

Handmade pasta prepared fresh each morning from imported semolina
Prime steaks sourced for marbling, dry-aged on site
Italian and international wines, sommelier-curated
Private dining available for events, celebrations, and corporate meals
