Questions & Answers

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before joining us for an evening in the garden.

Do I need a reservation?

While walk-ins are welcome, we highly recommend making a reservation 3–5 days in advance, especially for weekend evenings and live jazz Fridays. You can book directly through our online reservation form or via WhatsApp.

What is your dress code?

We recommend smart casual or semi-formal attire to complement the elegant, candlelit atmosphere of our garden and dining rooms.

Do you host private events or large groups?

Yes, we have dedicated private dining spaces perfect for corporate events, celebratory dinners, proposals, and small gatherings. Please contact our team directly or note your request in the booking form.

Can you accommodate dietary requirements?

Absolutely. Our chefs can prepare vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, and dairy-free options. Please inform your server of any allergies or requirements, or leave a note when making your reservation.

Where is Cantine Divino located and is parking available?

We are located on Mabua Road in the quiet residential area of Kololo, Kampala. Secure, gated on-site parking is available for all restaurant guests.
